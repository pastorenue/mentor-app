What is in it for you?

You will...

  • have the opportunity to engage entrepreneurs of your choice and teach them how to grow their business

  • work on new and exciting challenges to overcome around team building, funding, product development, positioning, pivoting or business development, sales strategy and operations optimization
  • connect mentees to opportunities
  • exchange stories about failures, lessons, tips and tricks with thebossoffice community.
