Terms and Conditions For Mentees

  1. On this platform, a mentee is recommended to have a maximum of two business mentors in the course of one fiscal year
  2. The objective of this program is to help your business grow from its cradle to sustainability
  3. A mentee will one (1) hour a month from the mentor for one year in pursuance of the objective stated in (ii) above
  4. A mentee has the option of seeking to change a mentor after a genuine reason has been provided why the relationship must be terminated
  5. All meetings should be planned clearly towards the objective stated in (ii) above and on the curriculum that would be sent upon successful registration
  6. An evaluation sheet will be filled by the mentee after each meeting which would be sent to the mentor for validation
  7. Thebossoffice.com would contact you and your mentor concerning the progress of the mentorship when the need arises
  8. All meetings must be conducted in mutually agreed locations at agreed times.
  9. All meetings should be done in places that do not raise any concerns for both parties
  10. In cases of misconduct or anomalies, the mentee has the right to contact thebossoffice.com who would take up investigation upon receipt of such reports
  11. Mentees must not be pressured to patronize corporate services unless through recommendation from thebossoffice.com
  12. Mentees are free to consult thebossofice.com to provide business support services when needed
  13. Mentees are to respect the fundamental human rights of their mentor
  14. Thebossoffice.com has the right to terminate access, rights and membership to this mentorship programme after a written email/notification has been sent to the mentor/mentee in cases of necessity
  15. Upon decision to pull out of the mentorship programme, the mentee must write to thebossoffice.com stating reason(s) for the decision
  16. Mentees are not allowed to solicit on this platform
