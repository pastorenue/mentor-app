About Us

Thebossoffice was borne out of a strong need to salvage the prevalent economic problems in Nigeria and other African countries. From our findings, Nigeria has well over 40 million micro, small and medium business enterprises which employ over 80% of Nigeria’s labour force and contributes about 50% to Nigeria’s GDP yet our findings also reveal a 73% business death rate within this same sector within the first three years of operations. This, we also gathered that apart from poor funding options, was largely due to poor leadership and management skills the small business owners possessed.

Thus thebossoffice is set up to address the gaps and connect well meaning Nigerian industry veterans, mentors, experts and business owners to help startups and MSMEs to grow their businesses from infancy through to sustainability.

Our goal is to ensure that we reduce poverty and unemployment by strengthening the SMEs in Nigeria, Africa and the world.

We have a robust network of business mentors and experts from various sectors of Nigeria’s economy, business schools and other big economies around the world.

Mission Statement

Guiding startups and small businesses to a sustainable future through mentorship and support services.
