Business diagnostics

We thoroughly review business operations and the books to determine the health of the business and make recommendations. Apart from recommendation, we render services to SMEs in the following aspects;

Operations optimization

We offer operations optimization services to SMEs to improve their business processes to attain better efficiencies and optimum performances

Business templates

As part of the support services we render, business templates will be available for download on our website. These would be paid for by SMEs. The templates would cover all business aspects

Business plan

Most small businesses do not have business plans thus at the preliminary stages of the mentorship program, we encourage the mentors to demand for a business plan so that sustainability plan can be checked. To this end, we provide free templates and paid business plan services for SMEs

Marketing and brand communication

Our experts can transform your business from one ordinary business to the spotlight when we’re engaged. We have professional industry experts that can transform your business
